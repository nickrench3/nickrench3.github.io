About.

The Central Illinois Truck Show is hosted every year in Jacksonville, IL! We hope to see everyone there next year! The 2020 show date is TBA!

The Central Illinois Truck Show was founded in 2016 by Clint Stevens, Nick Rentschler and Quentin Reynolds. We have hosted 4 shows so far and have been involved in various community activities. We support any local car show and bring a group of trucks to every show that we can!
