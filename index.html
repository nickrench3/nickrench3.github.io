About

The Central Illinois Truck Show is hosted every year in Jacksonville, Illinois. The show features over 100 trucks, events including a show ‘n shine, dirt drags, burnout contests and a dyno. As well, various vendors display their products.

Come to the show and check out the custom pick up trucks from around the area. From old to new, there is a truck for everyone at this show. No matter what you have, bring it out!
